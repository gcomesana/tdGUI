In order to the app can be run in your environment, you must update your browser. To do this one:
  • Go to About Firefox by clicking on the Firefox menu on Mac or Help menu on Windows/Linux
  • The About Firefox window will open and Firefox will begin checking for updates and downloading them automatically.
  • That's it. When the updates are ready to be installed, click Restart to Update. Keep in mind this procedure will update the Firefox browser to the very newest version.
